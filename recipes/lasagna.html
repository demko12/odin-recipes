Lasagna

Lasagna (plural “lasagne”) is a flat and expanded pasta sheet, traditionally made in Italy with Parmigiano-Reggiano (Parmesan cheese),
Béchamel sauce (white sauce), and ragù (a meat-based sauce).

Ingredients

  • 1 pound sweet Italian sausage.
  • Quarter pound lean ground beef.
  • Cup of minced onion.
  • 2 cloves garlic, crushed.
  • 1 (28 ounce) can crushed tomatoes.
